    Frequently Asked Questions

    How does the auditing and implementation package works?
    The auditing and implementation package starts at $2000. This is the starting price for regular marketing websites. The prices changes based on the nature of business (SaaS or Ecommerce...) and the total number of pages to track as well as the total number of events to track. If you have any questions, please reach out to our sales team.
    Do you offer ongoing support for implementation?
    We don't necessarily offer any ongoing support after the implementation phase is done. However, if you do have a need for this, please reach out to our sales team and we can reach an understanding. Note that we do offer documentation for the work we conduct as well as offer help and support during the implementation phase.
    Do you offer audting and implementation services for mobile applications?
    Yes, we do. If you have an Android application, an iOS application or a cross-plarform application, we can help you track your app's performance and build a solid data foundation.
    Can I get only the auditing services without any implementation? Or vice versa?
    If you are looking for help auditing your current analytics set up, our consulting services are better suited for you. If you need help implementing web anaylicts on your website or website, please reach out to our sales team and they can help build a package most suited for your needs.
    Can I get a dashboard built without purchasing the auditing & implementation package?
    Yes, you can. Please check our data visualization services.
    How long does does it take to complete the audit and implementation?
    The time it takes to deliver the audit and implementation varies based on the size and complexity of the work. After the initial call, our team will be able to provide you with a timeline for both phases. We will share with you the timeline and make sure to start working once we have a signoff.
